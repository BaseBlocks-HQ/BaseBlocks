Access team settings from the dashboard by clicking your team name → **Settings**.

## General

**Name** — The display name for your team. Shown in the dashboard and navigation. Can be changed at any time without affecting URLs.

**Slug** — The subdomain used in all your site URLs:

```
https://your-slug.baseblocks.dev/site-name
```

<Callout type="warn">
  Changing the team slug changes the URL for every site in the workspace. Existing links and bookmarks will break. There is no automatic redirect.
</Callout>

## Members

The Members tab shows all current members and pending invitations.

**Invite a member** — Enter an email address and select a role. An invitation email is sent. The invitation expires after 7 days if not accepted.

**Change a role** — Click the role badge next to a member's name to select a new role. Takes effect immediately.

**Remove a member** — Click **Remove** next to a member's name. They lose access to all sites in the workspace immediately. Their content is not deleted.

**Revoke an invitation** — Click **Revoke** next to a pending invitation before it is accepted.
