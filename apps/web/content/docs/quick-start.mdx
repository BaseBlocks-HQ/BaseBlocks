Get a team workspace, create a site, build a page, and publish — all in a few clicks.

<Steps>
<Step>

## Create an account

Go to [baseblocks.dev](https://baseblocks.dev) and sign in with Google or Microsoft. No credit card required.

</Step>
<Step>

## Create a team

After sign-in you land on the dashboard. Click **New Team**, enter a name, and choose a slug.

Your slug becomes the subdomain for all sites in this workspace:

```
https://your-slug.baseblocks.dev
```

The slug must be lowercase and hyphen-separated. You can change it later in Team Settings.

</Step>
<Step>

## Create a site

Inside your team dashboard, click **New Site**. Give it a name and a slug.

Your site will be reachable at:

```
https://your-team.baseblocks.dev/your-site
```

</Step>
<Step>

## Open the editor

Click your site to open it. You land in the editor with an empty first page ready to build.

The editor has three areas:
- **Sidebar** — your page tree
- **Canvas** — the live page render
- **Element picker** — all available blocks and sections

</Step>
<Step>

## Add content

Click **Add layout** in the sidebar to insert a row. Then open the element picker and click any element to drop it into the layout slot.

Start with a **Heading** and a **Paragraph** to get something on the page.

</Step>
<Step>

## Publish

Click **Publish** in the editor header. Your draft goes live immediately at your site URL.

That's it. Your site is live.

</Step>
</Steps>
