## 1. What This Policy Covers

This Cookie Policy explains how BaseBlocks uses cookies and similar technologies on `baseblocks.dev`.

In this policy, **BaseBlocks** refers to the BaseBlocks business and website.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies allow a website to remember certain information for a period of time, such as language, interface state, or other usage preferences.

The site may also use similar mechanisms, such as local storage or session storage, to work properly.

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies We Currently Use

Based on the current product setup, BaseBlocks may use cookies or similar client-side storage to:

- keep users signed in
- preserve session behavior
- store language and interface preferences
- remember interface state
- remember layout preferences
- support navigation and core product functionality

These technologies are used because they are necessary to operate the website or deliver a feature you requested.
